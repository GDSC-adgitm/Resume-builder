    How to write a good resume ?

    • Keep a consistent, easily-readable format.
    • Do highlight your most relevant experiences.
    • Don't steal the job desscription's exact wording.
    • Qualify and quantify your experiences whenever possible.Include numbers and percentages when possible.
    • Don't go over two pages.
    • Don't include long, generic objective statements.
    • Within each section, you must list expereinces in reverse chronological order, starting with the most recent.
